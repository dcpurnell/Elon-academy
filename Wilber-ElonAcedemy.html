What is Elon Academy?

About the Program

Elon Academy is a college-access and academic enrichment program hosted at Elon University. It supports high‑potential students who want to grow academically, personally, and professionally.

The program helps scholars prepare for college, build confidence, and develop leadership skills that last a lifetime.

Who Are the Scholars?

Scholars are motivated high school students selected for their dedication, character, and desire to succeed. They participate in workshops, classes, and campus activities designed to help them reach their goals.

What Happens on Campus?

Academic Classes

Scholars attend classes taught by Elon faculty and staff. These sessions strengthen skills in writing, math, science, leadership, and college readiness.

College Preparation

Students learn about college applications, financial aid, scholarships, and how to succeed in higher education. They also meet college mentors and advisors.

Campus Life

Scholars experience real campus life — eating in dining halls, staying in residence halls, exploring facilities, and participating in group activities.

Leadership & Community

Team-building events, service projects, and leadership workshops help scholars grow as individuals and as members of their communities.
