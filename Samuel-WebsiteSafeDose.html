What SafeDose does

The small, steady help that makes a routine stick.

SafeDose isn't complicated. It does a few useful things really well, so taking medication on time feels less like one more thing to remember.

Reminders at your times

Personal text reminders sent at exactly the times you set, in a warm, plain-spoken tone — not a robotic alarm.

A simple check-in

Reply to confirm a dose was taken. If there's no reply after a while, SafeDose sends one gentle follow-up.

Optional family heads-up

With everyone's permission, a family member can get a quiet note when a dose is confirmed — for a little peace of mind.

Help setting up your week

We'll help you lay out a clear weekly schedule the first time, so every reminder lands at the right moment.

How it works

Three steps, then it runs quietly in the background.

1

Tell us the schedule

Share the medication times you want reminders for and who (if anyone) should get a heads-up. We'll set it up with you.

2

Get friendly reminders

At each time you chose, a warm text arrives. Reply when you've taken it, and SafeDose marks it done.

3

Stay on track together

Miss a reply and we'll send one gentle nudge. If you've opted in, your chosen family member gets a quiet note.

Good to know before you start

Honest about what SafeDose is — and isn't.

!

Not for emergencies

If you or someone you care for is unwell or in danger, call 911 (or your local emergency number) right away. SafeDose can't respond to emergencies.

!

Not medical advice

SafeDose doesn't give health advice or change anything about your medication. Questions about your medicine belong with your doctor or pharmacist.

!

A helpful aid, not a guarantee

Text messages can be delayed or missed. SafeDose is a helpful backup for your memory — please keep other safeguards in place too, like a pill organizer or pharmacy auto-refills.

!

Your information stays private

We only collect what's needed to send your reminders, we never sell it, and we only share a dose confirmation with the family contact you've approved.

About SafeDose

Started by Samuel Tyderek, a 15-year-old who wanted to help.

I’m Samuel Tyderek, a student and founder from North Carolina, and I started SafeDose because I noticed how many seniors and busy families struggle to keep medication on schedule — and how much stress that adds to everyone who loves them.

My goal is simple: make staying on track feel easier and kinder. SafeDose is a reminder service built around real people, not a hospital system. It doesn't replace doctors, pharmacists, or good habits — it just gives them a gentle, reliable hand.

I’m building this carefully, with grown-ups I trust helping me along the way, because the people SafeDose is for deserve something done right.
