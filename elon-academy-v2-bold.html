What we do

Built for students who deserve a shot.

Part of Elon University's Center for Access and Success, the Academy serves students with financial need and/or no family history of college — and it costs families nothing. Nationally, only 14% of low-income students finish college. Elon Academy scholars finish at 79%.

🎓

3 Summers on Campus

Live in the dorms, eat in the dining halls, learn in real college classrooms — every summer of high school.

🗓️

Saturday Academy

Year-round Saturday sessions for scholars and their families — prep, skills, and community.

🤝

Mentors & Coaches

College mentors and academic coaches in your corner from day one through graduation.

The roadmap

Five steps to a degree.

A multi-phase journey that starts freshman year of high school and doesn't stop until you walk across a college stage.

1

Apply

Freshman-year students in Alamance County apply and join as scholars.

2

Summer Residencies

Three summers living and learning on Elon's campus.

3

Saturday Programs

Year-round sessions with your family alongside you.

4

Transition to College

The summer after graduation, get ready for the leap.

5

College Success

Support continues on your college campus until you finish.

By the numbers

Scholars who show up.

Every high school scholar takes at least one honors or AP class. Average unweighted GPA: 3.66. Eleven scholars have earned Gates-Millennium, Golden Door, Morehead-Cain, or Cheatham-White scholarships.

📚

100% Honors/AP

Every scholar is enrolled in at least one honors or AP course.

🏆

Elite Scholarships

🏠

NC Proud

96% of scholars attend North Carolina colleges and universities.
